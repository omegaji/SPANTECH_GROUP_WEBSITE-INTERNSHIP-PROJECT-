SRP 100

Vertical moving platform for onsite works like plastering, painting, cladding etc. to replace conventional scaffolding.

  • SRP 100, THE MOST ADVANCED AND THE SAFEST PLATFORM YOU CAN ASK FOR!
Download Brochure

  • Capacity Chart
‘W’ TYPE FRAME:

Special ‘W’ Type’ frame gives access to labour even at building corners. This ensures that, with minimum distance between roof supporting devices, you can use entire 7.5 mtrs. platform length.

ROPE GUIDE PULLEY & ROPE LOCKING DEVICE:

New design Rope Guide pulley is provided on the roof device for easy pulling of the wire rope on top. A new rope locking device is also provided on the roof device middle beam to lock it and the wire rope is crimped with aluminium

HOT DIP GALVANIZATION OF CRUCIAL COMPONENTS:

Hot dip galvanization of crucial components like Platform, Pins & Top Mechanism increases the life & benefits usage during applications like plastering etc.

Safety Harness BELTS:

With each SRP, 2 Nos. of Safety Harness Belt with Rope and Hook is provided for the workers’ safety.

CONTROL PANEL:

Control panel body is made of good finish stainless steel which gives longer life. It contains all electrical components of Schneider, the ELCB & contractors. 3 pin Electric power socket of European design on bottom of panel is also provided.

CABLE PROTECTING DEVICE:

The cable protecting device is provided which stops the platform when the cable is pulled, thus increasing the life of the cable. The cable is of 100 m. & properly packed in wooden box.
